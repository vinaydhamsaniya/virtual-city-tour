Early History

Ahmedabad was originally known as Karnavati, established in the 12th century by King Karna of the Solanki dynasty.

Founding of Ahmedabad (1411 AD)

In 1411, Sultan Ahmed Shah of the Muzaffarid dynasty founded Ahmedabad, constructing forts, mosques, and markets.
